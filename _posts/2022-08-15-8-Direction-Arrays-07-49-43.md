  Arrays of zonohedral cells in eight directional system.  Major cells are 56-faced polar zonohedra, connecting along polar axis at shared vertices, and arranged in a ring of 8 cells, sharing faces with adjacent cells. Space between the central chain of cells and the surrounding ring is filled with dodecahedral and hexahedral cells, with all edges being unit lengths perallel with some of the 8 coordinate directions.  

This is a continuation of the series shown in [7 Directional Space Filling](https://john-kostick.github.io/vzome-sharing/2022/06/07/7-D-space-filling-15-03-11.html)
