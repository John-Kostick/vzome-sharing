This shows [Catalan Solids](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Catalan_solid) and their Archimedean Duals. Specificaly, it is about constructing these polyhedra with vZome. This project started with some queries and discussions about how to build thhem. Catalans can be built from midpoints of edges of their Archimedean duals as follows. These midpoints are generally equidistant from a center point, which is to say, they lie on a sphere.  By constructing a cross product of a radius to a midpoint, and the edge, a strut is made that is tangent to the sphere.  Intersections of these struts define vertices of the polyhedron.  This series of models follows the sequence listed in the linked article, with Archimedean Duals as open frames.
