  A lattice composed of BCC (yellow struts) and Oct-Tet (green struts.) Polyhedra are 28-hedra, Truncated Tetrahedra, Tetrahedra, and hexagonal, rhombic, and triangular prisms. In this model, yellow and green strut lengths have been adjusted to be equal, so this array can be considered regular.
